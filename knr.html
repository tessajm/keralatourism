Kannur, more popularly known as Cannanore, is a northern district in Kerala famed for its vibrant culture and rich heritage. Sharing its borders with the Western Ghats and Arabian Sea, the region exhibits abundant natural beauty and cultural tradition.During ancient times, this district was a cultural, religious as well as a commercial hub of the Malabar region. Kannur owes its pulsating culture to several empires that vied to make this territory an abode of their power.
